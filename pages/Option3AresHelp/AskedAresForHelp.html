Take god Ares’ help

Unwilling to be a pawn of kings,
and with a lingering distrust of the other Olympian gods after his many trials,
Hercules decides to take matters into his own hands.
He journeys to the battlefield and,
in a moment of reckless desperation,
calls upon the aid of Ares, the brutal and unpredictable god of war.
Ares, a divine being who is more focused on bloodshed and chaos than strategy,
answers the summons — but his help comes at a terrible price.
